About my mindset

Being deeply passionate about Engineering and an ardent enthusiast in the field, I am driven to solve problems and find optimal solutions, particularly in Product Design & Development. Every project or tinkering endeavor I undertake is seen as an opportunity for continuous learning, pushing me towards limitless knowledge acquisition.

One of my primary goals is to incorporate low-cost Automation and Robotics, integrating effective mechanical mechanisms with electronics and firmware in my research and development projects. I strive to achieve efficient and seamless results, aiming for the highest standards such as 6 Sigma.

I hold great respect for experts in the field, regardless of their age, and am always eager to humbly seek guidance from them. By doing so, I am committed to delivering effective and efficient outcomes in my endeavors. Furthermore, I attribute the wonderful situations and opportunities that align with my desires to the grace of God, and I aim to lead a fulfilling and joyful life while also contributing positively to the lives of others through God's blessings.
